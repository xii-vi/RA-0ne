Avatar

Avatars are used to make to UI more interactive. Avatars are of various types. Here two types are available to use.

Standard Avatar

The avatar component has 4 sizes, avatar-xl, avatar-lg, avatar-std and avatar-sm

Avatars with status

Avatars support presence indicators. There are three indicators online, offline and dnd.
