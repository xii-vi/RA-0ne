Alert

Alert is a component used to draw attention towards important thing that is happening on a web page. Here are two alert type, a simple alert and another one with icons.

Standard Alerts

A simple success alert.
A simple danger alert.
A simple warning alert.
A simple info alert.

The alert component has four variants alert-success, alert-danger, alert-warning and alert-info

Alerts with icons

A simple success alert.
A simple danger alert.
A simple warning alert.
A simple info alert.

The alert component has four variants alert-success-icon, alert-danger-icon, alert-warning-icon and alert-info-icon
